Hot Chocolate Recipe

A cup of hot chocolate to warm the body and soul

Ingredients:

  • Hot chocolate mix
  • Milk or water
  • Mini marshmellows

Instructions:

  1. Heat your milk (or water)
  2. Pour your milk in a mug
  3. Pour your hot cholocate mix in the mug and stir
  4. Add marshmellows, take a pic for your socials, and enjoy~
